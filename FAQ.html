ORDER FLOW (How to place an Order?)


How do I place an order?
Simply navigate to your item(s) of choice, and select the color/size (if applicable) you want. Click the ADD TO CART button. Your items are now in your shopping cart!

Once you've added all the items you want to your cart, click on the cart icon at the very top of the page to view your cart.

Check that all the items in your cart are correct, and click CHECKOUT to proceed to payment. Follow the check out process by logging in (for returning customers) or registering i.e filling in your details manually (for new customers). For new customers, you will be asked to enter your shipping details. Post that you will be on the payments selection page where you can apply any COUPON CODE (if applicable) and then select your preferred mode of payment i.e. either COD (Cash on Delivery) or CREDIT CARD (for Credit Card, Debit Card & Netbanking through a secured payment gateway)

Click COMPLETE ORDER to place your Order. Once you've placed your order successfully, please check your email for your order confirmation. For those who have selected COD as the payment method, you will find instructions on "HOW TO CONFIRM YOUR COD ORDER?" in the order confirmation email. Check your spam/junk folder if you can't find the email in your inbox. Do contact us if you get confused at any point!

I am unable to place an Order online, what do I do?
Don't worry, our customer care team or even better, our Stylists will be able to guide you, recommend you and even place the Order on your behalf. Just call us at 08068970285 (10.00 A.M. to 7.00 P.M. from Monday to Friday) or alternately write to us at support@jokerandwitch.com.
