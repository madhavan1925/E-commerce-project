RETURN PROCESS OVERVIEW

STEP 1: Once you are sure you want to return the product, immediately Go to the RETURN YOUR ORDER section and generate a return request. Pls note all return requests need to be raised within 7 days of product delivery and all the above mandatory return criteria have to be met.

STEP 2: Our Customer Care team will review your Return request within 24- 48 working hours and a reverse pick up for the delivered product will be arranged.

STEP 3: Please make sure that you have packed the return product very safely (use Joker & Witch Original packaging box, or some strong packaging box with sealed tapes) to prevent any loss or damage to the product during transit. Any product received damaged in transit will not be qualified for refund and will be sent back to the customer at their expense. No Refund Coupon will be issued in such cases. In case a product is received without the packaging black box, we will be deducting an amount of Rs 99/- from the refund amount.

STEP 4: Hand over the parcel to our pick up agent.

The returned parcel will take 7 to 10 days to reach our warehouse. Your patience is highly appreciated.

Once we receive the returned product(s), a quality check will be performed on the items by our quality team. This is subject to the return having met all the above mentioned criteria of Return.

If the returned product(s) meets all the criterion for return, then we will initiate your refund within 24-48 working hours of receiving the parcel. You will receive an email with the Coupon code worth your product value.

In Case your Pincode is not serviceable by our courier partner for a reverse pick up. We will request you to send the products via any courier service or India Post so that the product reaches us safe. We will not be responsible for any damage to the parcel during transit, and will not be accepting any damaged products. Send us an email on support@jokerandwitch.com & share the tracking details of the courier with us. In such a case, an additional amount of Rs 99 will be added to your coupon refund value as a shipping reimbursement. This will not be in actuals.

Note: In the case of Monetary Refund, there will be a deduction of Rs. 75/- against the reverse logistics charges & the remaining amount will be refunded.

Pls note that the entire process of return , from the day of us receiving your email to you receiving the coupon code may take roughly between 10-15 days.
